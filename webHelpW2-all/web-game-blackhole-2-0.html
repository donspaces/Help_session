Game - Blackhole 4.0

Introduction

This game is a 2D arcade-like game. As a player, one need to control the black hole to move and consume different quarks in quantum space while avoiding other blackholes from eating itself. There are 4 types of quarks:

Positron: A strong positive charged particle that could make the black hole larger.

Tettron: A weak positive charged particle that could make the black hole larger.

Electron: A weak negative charged particle that could make the black hole smaller.

Muon: A strong negative charged particle that could make the black hole smaller.

Blackhole will start at 1E+000MeV/C^2 right after the game starts.

Control

Windows/MacOS

Use W (up), A (left), S (down), and D (right) to control the blackhole.

android/iOS

Drag on the screen to activate the virtual joystick, drag the circle inside the joystick, the blackhole will move in the corresponding direction.
